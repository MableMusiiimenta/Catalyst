The 7 Habits

I learnt about the seven habits of highly effective people by Stephen R.Covey.

  1. To be proactive: Focus and act on what you can control and influence instead of what you can't.
  2. To begin with the end in mind: Define clear measures of success and a plan to achieve them.
  3. To put first things first: Prioritize and achieve your most important goals instead of constantly reacting to urgencies.
  4. To think win-win:Collaborate more effectively by building high-trust relationships.
  5. Seek first to understand, then to be understood: Influence others by developing a deep understanding of their needs and perspectives.
  6. Synergize: Develop innovative solutions that leverage differences and satisfy all key stakeholders.
  7. Sharpen the saw: Increase motivation, energy, and work/life balance by making time for renewing activities.

Time matrix

I learnt about the quadrants of our time. I learnt that i've been spending a lot of time in Q4 by being on tiktok and watching lots of movies. I am especially working on that area and concetrating more on Q2 these days where i read books and articles that help me grow,am exercising more and eating healthy,doing my research about my classwork and other things new things that i come across on a daily. I am also working on my personal relationships with my friends, family and partner,i am investing in Q2 to avoid Q1.

Q2 is the goal

I've learnt about paradigms,something i had never reflected upon. My favorite take-away is how we handle hard situations with people around us, our classmates,workmates,friends, family and partners.

After these two weeks, I believe i am headed to the path of being a really "good person" lol. Currently, I am training myself to put all these great lessons i learnt into practice. Not that i was totally bad before these lessons,no, I was "alright", sort of, but life was hard with all emotions and attitudes involved on a daily. Now everything has been put to light and life is getting easier with this new knowledge. Also, i learnt that it's good practice to read/listen to books/podcasts about all life aspects,they help guide us through life's dynamics. Finally, I learnt that i am 100% in charge of my life, how i view things and of my decisions. Nobody is to blame! I now aim for proactivity in all aspects of my life instead of sitting around and be reactive as i wait for magic to happen and change my situation.
